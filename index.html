Real-Time Workforce Attendance Tracking

Monitor your workforce attendance instantly and accurately.

VeriTrack enables organizations to track employee check-ins and check-outs in real time using AI-powered QR codes and liveliness checks. This ensures every attendance record is authentic, secure, and available for immediate review by management.

Payroll Integration

Seamlessly connect attendance data with payroll processing.

Automatically extract data from Weekly and Monthly Overview reports to streamline payroll calculations. Reduce manual errors and administrative workload by integrating accurate attendance records directly into your payroll system.

Visitors Attendance Tracking

Track and manage visitor attendance with ease.

This feature is available upon request as part of an extra plan. It allows organizations to monitor and record visitor check-ins and check-outs, enhancing security and compliance for your premises.

General Workforce Growth

Empower your staff with tailored daily career and growth tips.

Each employee receives daily tips and guidance specifically tailored to their department and role. This supports ongoing professional development, encourages best practices, and helps staff grow in their careers while contributing to overall organizational success.
